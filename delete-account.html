Delete your PowRangrs account

Last updated: April 14, 2026

You can request deletion of your PowRangrs account and all associated data at any time. Everything is handled manually — there's no automated form to fill out. Just send a short email from the Google account you signed in with and we'll take care of it.

Request account deletion

What gets deleted

  • Your account record (Google email, display name, profile picture URL, Google account identifier)
  • Your linked Steam64 ID (if you linked one)
  • Your session invites and all responses you've made (accept / decline / maybe)
  • Your FCM device tokens (you'll stop receiving PowRangrs push notifications immediately)
  • Any cached CS2 match stats keyed to your Steam account

What may be retained

  • Aggregate session data your teammates created (e.g. a session you RSVP'd to will remain visible to them, minus your response). Your name will be removed from the response list.
  • Routine server request logs — rotated automatically by the hosting provider. These do not contain personal data.

How to request it

  1. Click the button above, or email tkoshkin.dev@gmail.com manually.
  2. Send from the Google account email address you use to sign in to PowRangrs, so we can match the request to your account.
  3. You'll receive a confirmation reply within a few days. Your account and data are typically deleted within 7 days of the request.

Questions

If you have any questions about what gets deleted, or you want only some of your data removed, mention it in the email and we'll work it out with you. See the full privacy policy for more detail on what we collect and why.
